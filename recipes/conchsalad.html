Conch Salad

The explosion of citrus and hot pepper flavours along with freshly diced veggies and tasty white conch meat are what make every spoonful of this local delicacy delightful to both the eye and palate.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of conch
  • 1 regular cucumber, chopped
  • 2 roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1 orange, juiced
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Onions
  • 1 Scotch bonnet pepper, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • Black pepper and salt to taste
  • Cilantro, chopped, to garnish

Instructions

  1. Soak conch in water and lemon juice for about 10 minutes.
  2. Wash and remove any debris, slime, or black pieces.
  3. On a good surface, cover meat with plastic wrap, using a meat tenderizer, pound conch thinly.
  4. Chop into bite-sized pieces.
  5. In a large non-reactive mixing bowl, add onions, lime juice, orange juice, bell pepper, tomatoes, onions, cucumber, conch meat.
  6. Mix till combined, add salt and black pepper to taste.
  7. Add hot pepper and cilantro.
  8. Cover the bowl and let sit for 10 minutes in the refrigerator.
